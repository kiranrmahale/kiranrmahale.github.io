About

Dr Kiran Mahale is a multidisciplinary scientist working on sustainable carbon materials, biomass conversion technologies, and energy storage materials. His work focuses on transforming agricultural waste into high-value materials for advanced energy, environmental, and circular economy applications.

Research Areas

Nutraceutical and Bioactive Compound Extraction

Extraction of nutraceuticals and bioactive compounds from agricultural and food processing waste.

Sustainable Carbon Materials

Development of high-performance carbon materials derived from agricultural biomass.

Energy Storage Materials

Carbon materials for lithium-ion batteries, sodium-ion batteries, and hybrid capacitors.

Biomass Conversion Technologies

Innovative processes converting biomass into functional carbon materials.

Circular Economy and Waste Valorisation

Transforming agricultural waste into valuable materials and sustainable technologies.
